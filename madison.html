Fun In Madison

Listed below are some of our favorite places within walking distance of our ceremony and reception. You can also check out this site for more ideas.


Himal Chuli - Our Favorite Madison Restaurant

Serving Himalayan/Nepalese cuisine, this place can't be beat if you want to try something new. Order the combination platter and you won't be disappointed. Heads up - it's a cash only restaurant.

318 State St
Madison, WI 53703

The Great Dane - Best Pub Food in Madison

Website

Along with their assortment of their very own microbrews, The Great Dane offers great pub food with class. Meals range from burgers and fries to Tandoori chicken. Great outdoor patio in the back as well.

123 East Doty St
Madison, WI 53703

Memorial Union Terrace

Website

The go-to spot for summer in Madison. Enjoy beer or ice cream as you watch the boats sail by.

800 Langdon St
Madison, WI 53706

Farmers' Market at the Capitol

Website

Take a walking tour around the capitol square and check out all that the farmers have to offer. Runs from 6AM to 2PM on Saturday. Try the honey from the guy who wears a honeycomb on his head.

2 East Main St
Madison, WI 53703

Madison Museum of Contemporary Art

Website

Located right next door to the Overture center (where the reception will be held), MMoCA is a small museum housing contemporary art exhibits and offering free admission.

227 State St
Madison, WI 53703

Wisconsin State Capitol

Website

Wisconsin's State Capitol Building. Check out the observation deck on the 6th Floor (accessible from 4th floor via NW or W stairways) for a 360 degree view of downtown.

2 East Main st
Madison, WI 53703
